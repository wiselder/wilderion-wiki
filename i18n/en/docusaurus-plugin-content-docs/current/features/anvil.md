### How It Works

In vanilla Minecraft, renaming an item on an anvil can cost many levels, especially for enchanted items. On our servers:

| Action | Vanilla | On Wilderion |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| Renaming a regular item | 1+ levels | **1 level** |
| Renaming an enchanted item | 5-39 levels | **1 level** |
| Renaming after repair | Accumulates | **1 level** |

<details>

No! Each renaming costs exactly 1 level, regardless of the item's history.

Yes, this improvement works on all Wilderion servers.
