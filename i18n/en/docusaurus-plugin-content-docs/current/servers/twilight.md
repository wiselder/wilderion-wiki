## âš”ï¸ Features

### World Without Borders and Coordinates

Forget about `F3`, chunk grid, and hitboxes. We're bringing back that magic of first entering the game: when you navigate by stars, landscape, and man-made trails.

:::note Mod Restrictions
Minimaps and Litematica work with restrictions, maintaining a fair challenge.
:::

**Explore the world through the eyes of a pioneer, not a topographer.**

### Living Economy and Resource Value

We've cut drops from natural spawn farms:
- Creepers
- Endermen
- Iron golems

**Result:** every ingot and every stack of gunpowder has weight.

:::tip How to Get Resources
- Develop manual farms
- Use spawners
- Trade with other players

Resources will become valuable again, not junk in chests.
:::

### Hardcore Villager Trading

Villages matter again! We use a biome enchantment system: to collect a top set, you'll have to cross the entire world.

**Features:**
- Forget about level V books from one villager
- Combine resources
- Use diamonds to buy equipment

### Nether as Art, Not a Highway

:::warning Nether Roof is Closed
We're returning you inside the Nether, so that every hub is part of the distorted biome's decorations, not a boring corridor on bedrock.
:::
