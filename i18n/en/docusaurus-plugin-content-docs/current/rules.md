# Project Rules

:::note Our Philosophy
Wilderion is a **server of friends**. We build a community based on mutual respect and trust. The main rule: *treat others as you want to be treated*.
:::

## üìú General Provisions

:::warning Important
By playing on our server, you automatically agree to all its rules.
:::

- Rules are universal for every project in the Wilderion server network (except for unique restrictions of individual projects)
- Ignorance of the rules does not exempt from responsibility
- Playing on any of the projects means your automatic agreement with these rules

### Administration Rights and Responsibilities

- Administration reserves the right to impose penalties and determine their severity independently
- Administration may not return items lost after rollback, for technical reasons, or items that disappeared as a result of a player ban
- Administration is obliged to notify about any changes in the rules through news channels in the project's social networks

## 2. Gameplay

### 2.1 Griefing

‚ùå **Prohibited:**
- [Griefing](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Griefing) in any form
- PVP without explicit consent of both parties
- Detonating crystals, respawn anchors, TNT in the overworld (except for clearing territory and creating farms)
- Being on private territories with marking signs without permission
- Using mechanisms, checking chests and similar vandalism in a player's personal space without permission

:::info Fines
The owner of private territory has the right to collect a fine if you refuse to leave after the first warning.
:::

### 2.2 World Cleanliness

- ‚ùå Prohibited to build and break objects, change spawn buildings without administration participation
- ‚úÖ Clean up after yourself: finish chopping trees, fill in creeper holes

:::tip Remember
Spawn is the face of the server!
:::

### 2.3 Bugs and Dupes

‚ùå **Prohibited** to use bugs and dupes for harm or personal gain.

**Examples of prohibited dupes:**
- 0-tick
- Dragon egg and falling blocks
- And others

### 2.4 Prohibited Software

:::danger Strictly Prohibited
Any programs that give an advantage:

**Clients:** Ares, Wurst, Impact, Matix, etc.

**Features:** Killaura, X-ray, ESP, Flight, Baritone, AutoFish, etc.

**Resource packs:** Transparent textures, ore highlighting, effect visibility changes, fullbright
:::

### ‚úÖ Allowed Modifications

- Optimization (Sodium, Optifine, Lithium)

- Simple Voice Chat
- Shaders

## 3. Farms

:::info
We don't prohibit building farms of almost any format until they interfere with server operation.
:::

### Farm Usage Rules

1. **Take only your resources** from public farms. Taking others' resources is considered theft.

2. **Don't modify others' farms** without permission. If you broke it ‚Äî discuss repair conditions with the owner or pay a fine.

3. **Plan before building.** Don't clutter the world with non-working or "disposable" farms.

:::warning Important
Leaving a farm broken without informing the owner is considered griefing!
:::
